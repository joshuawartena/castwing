What We Do

Marketing that moves
with intention

01.

Brand Strategy

Before an ad goes live or a Google listing is written, we figure out who you are and who you're talking to. This means defining your position, identifying your voice, and making sure your business means what you want to the right audience — not everything to everyone.

02.

Digital Marketing

From email campaigns to paid media to website organization, we build programs that are intentional, measurable, and designed around how your customers actually make decisions and how you operate your business.

03.

Content & Storytelling

Every person and business has a story worth telling. We help you find it, shape it, and put it in front of the right people — through web copy, social pages, emails, and campaigns that sound like you at your best.

04.

Local Digital Presence

When someone searches for what you do in your area, you need to show up — and show up correctly. We manage the local signals that matter: Google Business Profile, map visibility, local SEO, and location consistency for a healthy digital footprint.

05.

Organic & Paid Google

Google is where people turn for local intent. We build search strategies that work both sides — websites built on solid technical foundations, organic SEO best practices, and paid campaigns engineered for efficiency. Avoid wasted spend, keyword stuffing, and passive ad strategy.

06.

Robust Reporting

Marketing only works if you understand what's being done. Every plan includes clear, plain-language explanations — no vanity metrics or dashboards you need a decoder ring to read. Just honest summaries of what moved, what didn't, and what's next.

The Story Behind the Work

Built on the belief that small businesses deserve great marketing

I grew up watching my father run a small construction business — quoting jobs, handling the work, managing invoices, and doing it all without the marketing support bigger companies take for granted. He was excellent at his work and had to figure out the rest on his own. That drive stuck with me, and good work deserves to be found.

Over the past decade I've built and led digital marketing programs for franchise groups, a nationally-recognized mountain resort, and a regional restaurant chain — learning what actually drives customer engagement and visibility. Castwing is how I bring that experience to businesses that need a strategic partner, not just a vendor. No mystery metrics, no shady reporting, just clear thinking, honest work, and digital marketing that earns its keep as a tool in your toolbox.

Joshua Wartena
Founder, Castwing Marketing
